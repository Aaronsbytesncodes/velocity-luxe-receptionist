# Velocity Luxe Media — Cold Call Script

## Opening (gatekeeper or owner)

> "Hey, is this [Owner name]? … This is [Your name] with Velocity Luxe Media — not a big deal, I'll be quick. You guys do [plumbing/HVAC/etc] in [city], right?"

## Pain hook

> "When you're on a job and a new customer calls, what usually happens — voicemail, or someone actually picks up?"

*Listen. Don't talk over them.*

## Bridge

> "Most contractors I talk to say they're losing 2–3 jobs a week just from missed calls. We built an AI that answers like your front office, asks the right questions, and books real appointments on your calendar — then texts you the lead."

## Offer

> "We're running **7-day free trials** for [city] contractors. I can set you up in about 5 minutes on this call, give you a number to test, and you can call it yourself right now. If it doesn't book a fake appointment in the next 10 minutes, no hard feelings."

## Live demo close

1. Open `/onboarding` — fill their info
2. Connect Cal.com (or use demo slots)
3. Call the Vapi number together
4. Book a slot → show SMS on their phone

> "See that text? That's every lead you'll stop missing. After 7 days it's **$497 a month** for 24/7 coverage — one emergency job you would've missed pays for the whole year. Want to start the trial?"

## Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "What's one missed emergency call worth — $500, $2,000? We're $497/mo. That's one job." |
| "Competitor is cheaper" | "Cheap answering services take messages. We book real appointments on your calendar." |
| "Customers hate AI" | "They hate voicemail more. This sounds natural — you heard it." |
| "I have an office person" | "Perfect for nights, weekends, and overflow when they're busy." |
| "Send me info" | "Happy to — takes 5 min to show live vs an email. Got 5 min now?" |

## Follow-up (no close)

Text within 10 minutes:

> "Hey [Name], [You] from Velocity Luxe Media — here's the demo line we talked about: [number]. Call it anytime this week, free trial. Reply STOP to opt out."
